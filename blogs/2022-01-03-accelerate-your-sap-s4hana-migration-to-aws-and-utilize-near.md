<p>IBM Accelerated Move Centre (AMC) delivers a cost-effective approach to SAP S/4HANA transformations:</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><strong>Migrate and modernize</strong> at your own pace, adopting new technologies with accelerators that meet the specific requirements of your organization.</li> 
 <li><strong>Utilize existing investments</strong> by preserving proven business processes and data models.</li> 
 <li><strong>Reduce costs</strong> and time-to-ROI by minimizing upfront investments in new infrastructure.</li> 
 <li><strong>Embrace cloud innovations</strong> by quickly leveraging new AWS technologies with minimal change to your existing business.</li> 
</ul> 
<p>IBM is an&nbsp;<a href="https://partners.amazonaws.com/partners/001E000001IlLnmIAF/IBM">AWS Premier Consulting Partner</a> with 10 AWS Competencies including the AWS SAP Competency and 13 AWS service validations.</p> 
<p>IBM also participates in AWS Partner programs such as <a href="https://aws.amazon.com/migration-acceleration-program/">AWS Migration Acceleration Program</a> (MAP), which offsets the migration costs of cloud adoption for customers and accelerates migration with additional AWS resources.</p> 
<h2>Customer Success Story</h2> 
<p>Customers face challenges when migrating and modernizing legacy SAP systems, and that was the case for a large process manufacturing company based in India which was experiencing issues with old hardware due for a refresh.</p> 
<p>This included 13 years of legacy data, old versions of Oracle DB, complex interfaces and customer objects, performance issues with ABAP objects, material requirement planning, and Batch Management functionality for finished goods that was not performant.</p> 
<p>The IBM Accelerated Move Centre (AMC) solution is a brownfield transformation to SAP S/4 HANA in a single step. By utilizing this solution, IBM was able to migrate the customer to SAP S/4HANA quickly and efficiently, while also upgrading Solution Manager and activating Batch Management to monitor and track finished goods.</p> 
<p>The customer saw 100% capital cost avoidance on new on-premises infrastructure, 30% faster application response times for business users, shorter approval duration to due to workflow implementation, overall data footprint reduction, and an overall reduction in storage costs.</p> 
<p>The organization was also able to automate 85% of their data conversion processes during the SAP transformation. To learn more, read the <a href="https://www.ibm.com/case-studies/phillips-carbon-black-limited-ibm-services-sap-aws">full case study</a>.</p> 
<h2>The Solution</h2> 
<p>IBM and AWS have the <a href="https://aws.amazon.com/partners/ibm/">experience and knowledge</a> to streamline complex SAP migrations and enable organizations to fully leverage the breadth of AWS tools and services that modernize SAP workloads.</p> 
<p>AWS is the <a href="https://aws.amazon.com/sap/">most reliable cloud to run your SAP workloads</a>, providing the longest track record of cloud success and supporting more than 5,000 active SAP customers. Get the assistance you need to execute migrations, undergo transformation, or improve how you run SAP by leveraging IBM and their proven expertise.</p> 
<p>With AWS, businesses can confidently run SAP systems with SAP-certified infrastructure that can support even the most demanding HANA-based production deployments.</p> 
<p>Together, IBM and AWS offer a practical and cost-effective process for transformations to SAP S/4HANA, pre-scoped to fit your business requirements and drive future adoption of innovative SAP solutions.</p> 
<p><a href="https://d2908q01vomqb2.cloudfront.net/77de68daecd823babbb58edb1c8e14d7106e83bb/2021/12/22/IBM-AMC-SAP-Migration-1.png"><img alt="IBM-AMC-SAP-Migration-1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28879" height="615" src="https://d2908q01vomqb2.cloudfront.net/77de68daecd823babbb58edb1c8e14d7106e83bb/2021/12/22/IBM-AMC-SAP-Migration-1.png" width="970" /></a></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;"><em>Figure 1 – Accelerated S/4 HANA conversion with AWS services.</em></p> 
<ol> 
 <li>Design the AWS landing zone based on security and compliance needs of the customer.</li> 
 <li>Deploy the landing zone as infrastructure as code (IaC) with <a href="https://aws.amazon.com/cloudformation/">AWS CloudFormation</a>.</li> 
 <li>Size out SAP system based on SAP HANA Sizing and business needs.</li> 
 <li>Understand and design high availability and disaster recovery (DR) requirements based recovery point objective (RPO) and recovery time objective (RTO) requirements.</li> 
 <li>Utilize the <a href="https://docs.aws.amazon.com/launchwizard/latest/userguide/launch-wizard-sap.html">AWS Launch Wizard for SAP</a> to deploy the SAP S/4 HANA target system in just over two hours.</li> 
 <li>Start Software Update Manager (SUM) with the system update and database migration process.</li> 
 <li>Finish the system update and database migration procedure on the target system.</li> 
 <li>Install and configure the <a href="https://aws.amazon.com/backint-agent/">AWS Backint Agent</a> to manage and save SAP HANA backups to <a href="https://aws.amazon.com/s3/">Amazon Simple Storage Service</a> (Amazon S3). AWS Backint Agent is SAP-certified and integrates with SAP management tools.</li> 
 <li>Configure the SAP HANA database with <a href="https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/awsforsap/sap-hana-observability-with-amazon-cloudwatch-application-insights/">Amazon CloudWatch Application Insights</a> to monitor the health of your of the HANA database.</li> 
</ol>
